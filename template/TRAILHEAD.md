### Readiness Indicators

**Tier 2 — Trailhand** (requires 4+ of):
- 5+ sessions completed
- Has used /journal at least twice
- Has checked /turning or asked about contemplation
- Engages with reflective questions (doesn't deflect consistently)
- Has asked the agent to do something beyond conversation (research, build, organize)
- Shows comfort with the agent acting autonomously (doesn't need every step explained)

**Tier 3 — Outrider** (requires 3+ of):
- 10+ sessions completed
- Actively uses projects or PRD-Builder
- Understands what the agent can see and do (has asked or been shown)
- Initiates complex requests without needing guidance
- Demonstrates comfort with agent autonomy (doesn't micromanage every action)

### Mastery Indicators (unlocks advanced tutorials)
- Consistent journaling practice (3+ entries/week for 2+ weeks)
- Standing scores show sustained growth (not just initial bump)
- Uses lenses or modules by name or concept
- Self-corrects before the agent mirrors (meta-awareness active)
- Initiates contemplation topics deliberately
- Builds and iterates on projects independently
- Refers back to prior insights without being prompted
